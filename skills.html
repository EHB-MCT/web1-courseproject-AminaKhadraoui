Discoverer Metalbending

Toph is credited with discovering Metalbending, a groundbreaking technique that allows her to bend metal by sensing its trace amounts of earth within, breaking through limits previously thought impossible for Earthbenders.

Toph Beifong discovered Metalbending during a critical moment when she was facing a challenge in her journey. While imprisoned in a metal cage by the Fire Nation, she realized that the metal surrounding her was not just an unbendable substance but contained small traces of earth. By focusing on those vibrations, she was able to manipulate the metal for the first time.

Great sense of hearing

Despite being blind, Toph has an extraordinary sense of hearing, allowing her to detect vibrations and “see” the world through sound. She can identify voices and never forgets a tone, giving her a powerful advantage in combat and strategy.

In the search for the thieves that stole appa, Toph encountered the man responsible. She immediately recognized his voice, which led to her iconic quote: "I never forget a voice."
